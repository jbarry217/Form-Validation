First name should begin with an uppercase letter.
Last name should begin with an uppercase letter.
Social Security should be in the following format (ex. 123-45-6789 or 123.45.6789 or 123456789).
Email should be in the following format (ex. user@domain.com|net|org) where user and domain can be alphanumeric characters.
Username should be 7-12 alphanumeric characters and must contain at least 1 number.
Password should contain at least 1 uppercase letter, at least 1 lowercase letter, at least one number, at least 1 special character and should be between 9 and 15 characters long.
